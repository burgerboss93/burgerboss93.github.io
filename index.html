Bio

Often found with coffee in hand, New Jersey based singer/songwriter, Lane Simkins, offers listeners an honest and raw experience through his fresh lyricism and musicality. With a sound that portrays a 100% USDA certified organic gluten free indie/folk/ rock vibe, Lane's tunes tell real stories that provide a transparent glimpse of life's struggles and wins.
